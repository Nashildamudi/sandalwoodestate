Our Commitment to Quality

At an altitude of 1050 meters in Suntikoppa, Coorg, Karnataka, India, our shade-grown coffee epitomizes the intersection of tradition and quality. Established in 1892 and family-managed since 1949, Sandalwood Estate has earned its reputation both locally and internationally, with our premium beans delighting coffee enthusiasts worldwide.

Indulge in the rich legacy and exquisite flavors of Sandalwood Estate coffee – a true reflection of Coorg’s celebrated coffee heritage and timeless craftsmanship.

Welcome to Sandalwood Estate

Nestled in the heart of the premier coffee growing region, Sandalwood Estate offers the perfect environment for cultivating premium, high-quality coffee. Its rich soil, ideal climate, and expert farming practices come together to produce coffee that stands out for its exceptional flavor and aroma.

Corporate Social Responsibility

At Sandalwood Estate, our workers are the heart of our success. We are deeply committed to their well-being, fostering a safe, supportive, and empowering environment through initiatives such as:

  • Fair wages and ethical practices.
  • Access to essential resources.
  • Opportunities for professional and personal growth.

By prioritizing respect and care, we ensure that our legacy is not only about exceptional products but also about uplifting the lives of those who make it all possible.

Long-Term Vision

Sandalwood Estate is dedicated to the ongoing enhancement of sustainability, ensuring that the land we cherish remains fertile and vibrant for future generations. By harmonizing tradition with innovation, we strive to set a benchmark in sustainable agriculture, creating a legacy that honors both the environment and the people who call it home.

Sustainability Initiatives

Water Conservation

Through advanced water conservation systems, we prioritize the responsible and efficient use of this precious resource. From rainwater harvesting to eco-friendly irrigation methods, our sustainable practices are thoughtfully designed to preserve and protect water sources for generations to come.

Biodiversity Preservation

Our Estate thrives as a sanctuary for a diverse ecosystem, featuring majestic trees that have stood for over a century. We are deeply committed to preserving this rich biodiversity, fostering a harmonious balance between sustainable cultivation and environmental conservation.

Responsible Farming

We embrace eco-friendly farming practices that reduce our environmental footprint while preserving the integrity of our crops. From responsible soil management to sustainable harvesting techniques, every step reflects our commitment to environmental stewardship.

Certifications

Sandalwood Estate is EUDR compliant and proudly associated with:

  • At Source Certified - Olam Coffee
  • KPA - Karnataka Planters Association
  • CPA - Coorg Planters Association
  • UPASI - United Planters Association Of Southern India
  • SCAI - Speciality Coffee Association of India
  • BIOTA Coorg - A Farmer Produce Company
