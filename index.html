Welcome TO SANDALWOOD ESTATE COORG

Our Legacy of Coffee Cultivation

Located in the heart of Coorg’s primary Arabica coffee-growing zone, Sandalwood Estate specializes in producing exceptional washed coffees. We maintain a 70:30 ratio of Arabica to Robusta, ensuring a rich and diverse coffee portfolio. Each bean is cultivated with care to highlight the finest flavors, making every cup a testament to our dedication to excellence.

Beyond Coffee

Sandalwood Estate also cultivates premium black pepper and arecanut, showcasing our commitment to agricultural diversity. These plantations complement our coffee heritage and highlight our dedication to nurturing high-quality, sustainable crops.

A Heritage of Conservation

Our estate is home to a rich canopy of century-old trees, fostering biodiversity and ecological balance. We have implemented advanced water conservation systems, reflecting our responsibility towards sustainability and preserving the environment.

Experience the Spirit of Sandalwood

Sandalwood Estate is more than a coffee plantation—it’s a harmonious blend of tradition, sustainability, and quality. From our rich history to our commitment to the future, we invite you to join us in celebrating a legacy steeped in excellence. Discover the flavors, the stories, and the heart of Sandalwood Estate—a true testament to Coorg’s heritage and our passion for perfection.

Flavor and Brewing Experience

Great coffee starts with the right brewing method and the perfect balance of ingredients. Whether you prefer a bright and delicate cup or a bold and rich experience, the right approach makes all the difference.

For a smooth and flavorful brew, Arabica coffee shines when prepared using a pour-over method like a V60. With the right grind and a balanced coffee-to-water ratio, it brings out gentle citrus notes, hints of nuts, and a dark chocolate finish.

If you enjoy a stronger, more robust cup, Robusta coffee delivers a deep, full-bodied taste with rich cereal and nutty undertones. The right brewing technique highlights its bold character while keeping the flavors smooth and balanced.

You can also experiment with Aeropress, French Press, Moka Pot, or other brewing methods to match your personal taste preferences. The beauty of coffee lies in exploring different styles to find what suits you best.
